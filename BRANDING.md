# Proper Home Options Brand Direction

## Positioning

**Compare your options before deciding what to do with your property.**

Proper Home Options is a Michigan homeowner decision platform. It helps people
compare practical paths that may include:

- A direct cash sale
- A traditional listing
- A refinance conversation with a licensed mortgage professional
- Renting or holding the property
- Making repairs before selling
- Another practical solution based on the property and homeowner goals

The platform is led by Dimitri Kosmidis, a licensed Michigan real estate
professional and real estate investor. The operator's potential interest must remain visible: Dimitri or his
investor network may be interested in purchasing some properties, and Dimitri
may benefit from separately agreed real estate services.

## Brand Principles

- Clear before clever
- Helpful before persuasive
- Local and accountable
- Consumer-friendly and mainstream
- Transparent about incentives and professional roles
- Calm, practical, and free of urgency tactics
- Multiple paths rather than a predetermined cash-sale answer

## Visual Direction

- Warm neutral foundation
- Deep green for trust and primary actions
- Soft sage for information panels
- Restrained gold accent
- Editorial serif display type with a clean sans-serif interface type
- Real residential imagery rather than anonymous investor graphics
- Clean panels, modest corner radii, thin borders, and subtle depth

## Voice

Use direct, plain language. Explain tradeoffs without claiming that any path is
automatically correct. Avoid fear, pressure, exaggerated speed, comparative
superlatives, or promises of approval, value, or purchase.

## Required Disclosures

Proper Home Options is not an appraisal, lender, legal advisor, tax advisor, or
financial advisor. Homeowners should be encouraged to compare options and
consult qualified legal, tax, financial, mortgage, and real estate
professionals as appropriate.

Submitting information does not create an agency relationship or obligation to
sell, list, refinance, or use a particular provider. Any transaction or service
relationship requires separate written terms.

## Domain Direction

The final production domain remains undecided. Do not hardcode a new production
domain. `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` should be set in Vercel after domain selection,
clearance, and broker/compliance review.

Before launch, verify:

- Domain and social-handle availability
- Federal, state, and common-law trademark conflicts
- Michigan entity or assumed-name requirements
- Employing-broker advertising requirements
- A verified sender domain for transactional email
